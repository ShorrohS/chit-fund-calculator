How Chit Funds Work

What is a Chit Fund?

A chit fund is a savings and borrowing scheme where a group agrees to contribute fixed amounts regularly and take turns receiving the total corpus, with a discount.

How Chit Calling Works

When you "call" the chit, you receive the corpus amount minus a discount. The discount depends on demand and economic conditions.

Chit vs Bank FD

This calculator helps you find the breakeven amount - the minimum you need from your chit to match bank FD returns, accounting for the discount.
